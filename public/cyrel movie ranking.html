The Best Movies According to Cyrel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Forrest Gump

The movie is a touching tale of perseverance, love, and the American experience, with iconic moments and memorable quotes.

The Wolf of Wall Street

The film is a satirical commentary on the greed and excess of Wall Street in the 1990s, with intense performances and dark humor.

The Great Gatsby

The film explores themes of love, obsession, and the corrupting influence of wealth and power.
